Home Servers & Tech

I’ve always been fascinated by how things work - especially computers and networks. I love building home servers for personal cloud storage, game hosting, and media streaming. Setting up file sharing, managing backups, and experimenting with virtualization is something I genuinely enjoy.

My main computer is built for performance and multitasking. It runs on a Ryzen 7 9700X processor with 64GB of DDR5 RAM, dual 2TB Crucial P3 NVMe SSDs, and a 4070 Super GPU. I use a quad-monitor setup, which makes multitasking, monitoring systems, and working on creative projects incredibly efficient and enjoyable.

Planes and Rockets

I’m a huge aerospace nerd. What fascinates me most is the history and science behind flight and space exploration - from the Wright brothers to Saturn V rockets. I love learning how engineers overcame impossible challenges with limited technology, and how that shaped the world we live in. There's something inspiring about the constant push for innovation in aerospace.

Creative Projects

I enjoy building things from scratch, whether it’s a retro game emulator setup, a website, or a fictional universe. I like writing stories that blend tech, science fiction, and just a little humor. It’s a fun way to use my imagination and make people think - or laugh.

Over the last few years, I’ve also developed an interest in lockpicking - not for anything shady, but because it’s a fun mental challenge. It’s like solving a physical puzzle where every click and tension adjustment matters. I enjoy practicing with different locks and techniques to sharpen my focus and patience.
